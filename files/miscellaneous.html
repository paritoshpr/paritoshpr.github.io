    Bitcoins:A survey and comparison of parallel techniques

    As part of the course project in CSC8530 Parallel Algorithms at Georgia State, I wrote a survey paper on BITCOINS. I was interested in unravelling how it all works and explore the parallel and distributed computing perspectives for Proof-of-Work. This was back in 2014 when BITCOINS were still at $700 a pop! Miss those days! :D

    Data Visualization for US University Admissions and Outcomes

    A tree-map visualizing college outcomes based on data obtained from data.gov using D3Plus. A visualization tool I developed for college admissions to view graduating outcomes as part of CSE 6242 at Georgia Tech.
